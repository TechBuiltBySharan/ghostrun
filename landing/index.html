How it works

Three commands.
Full automation stack.

No YAML. No config files. No test framework to learn. Just your browser and three commands.

01 ⏺
Record in your real browser

ghostrun learn <url> opens Chrome. You click, fill, navigate naturally. Every action is captured as a replayable graph. No annotations needed.

02 ▶
Replay headlessly — anytime

ghostrun run <flow-name> replays your flow with Playwright — no browser visible. Screenshots every step, heals broken selectors automatically, tracks pass / fail over time.

03 ✦
AI explains every failure

When a step fails, Ollama (local) or Claude analyzes what broke and why — in plain English. No digging through stack traces. Uses AI if available, works without it too.

For AI Agents

Flows know
who created them.

GhostRun tracks whether a flow was recorded by a human or generated by an AI agent. Agents can create, run, and reuse flows autonomously via MCP — while humans can verify and promote them to trusted status.

An agent books a hotel once, records the flow, and replays it every week without human intervention. A human records the login flow once — every agent on the team can reuse it with their own credentials via --var flags.

👤
Human-recorded flows
Captured in a real browser by a person. High-fidelity, verified interactions. Selectors chosen from actual page state.
createdBy: human
🤖
Agent-generated flows
Created by AI from natural language, code scanning, or BFS exploration. Ready to run — humans can review and verify.
createdBy: agent
